Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 7, 2025

Introduction

HomeworkAI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application for AI-powered homework assistance and educational support.

Information We Collect

Personal Information:

We do not collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

Academic Content:

HomeworkAI processes your homework questions, problems, and academic content to provide AI-powered assistance. This content is processed securely and is not stored permanently on our servers.

Usage Data:

We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our AI models and app performance. This data is aggregated and cannot be linked to individual users.

Local Storage:

The app stores your preferences locally on your device, including:

  • Study preferences and subject settings
  • App interface customizations
  • Saved problems and solutions
  • Progress tracking and study history
  • Notification preferences

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide AI-powered homework assistance and solutions
  • Improve our AI models and educational algorithms
  • Personalize your learning experience
  • Track your academic progress and achievements
  • Provide relevant study recommendations
  • Maintain app functionality and performance

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your academic content to third parties. We may share aggregated, anonymous data for research purposes to improve educational technology.

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

Data Security

Your academic content is processed using enterprise-grade encryption and security measures. We implement industry-standard security protocols to protect your data.

Data Retention

Academic content is processed in real-time and not stored permanently. Your app preferences and progress data are stored locally on your device until you delete the app or reset your settings.

Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for educational use by students of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Third-Party Services

HomeworkAI may use third-party AI services for content processing. These services are bound by strict privacy agreements and do not retain your academic content.

AI Processing

Our AI technology processes your homework questions to provide educational assistance. This processing is done securely and your content is not used to train other users' models.

App Store Compliance

HomeworkAI complies with both Apple's App Store and Google Play Store guidelines and privacy requirements:

  • Transparent data collection practices
  • Secure data processing and storage
  • No unauthorized data sharing
  • Age-appropriate content and features
  • Cross-platform privacy consistency

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data (by uninstalling the app)
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns
  • Request information about our data practices
  • Opt out of data collection where applicable

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website and updating the "Last updated" date above.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@homeworkai.app
  • Website: https://homeworkai.app
